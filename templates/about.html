Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Tanzim Ikram Sheikh from Bangladesh. I'm a university student majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. Beyond programming and coding, I have a passion for reading and collecting books. Although my collection is small now, my dream is to own a vast personal library. I also enjoy tinkering with gadgets, taking photos, making videos, and traveling.

What is Tanzim's Virtual Bookshelf?

As a book enthusiast, I am deeply attached to my bookshelf. I often ponder what interesting things I can do with my books and the shelf that holds them. This curiosity led me to transform my ordinary bookshelf into an RGB-lit one, controllable via a smartphone over the internet. But that wasn't enough for me. I kept brainstorming and eventually came up with the idea of creating a web app to showcase and manage my books. Previously, I used Google Sheets to keep track of my collection—details like the titles, authors, page counts, languages, and whether I had read them. Now, with my app, managing my books is much more enjoyable and personalized!
